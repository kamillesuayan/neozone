NEO ZONE

NCT #127: THE 2ND ALBUM

Neo Zone is the second studio album by South Korean boy band NCT 127. The album was released on March 6, 2020, by SM Entertainment. It was preceded by the release of the song "Dreams Come True" on NCT 127 Day, January 27 (1/27), which was accompanied by its music video. On April 7, 2020 SM Entertainment announced that a repackage version of the album would be released in May of the same year.

Neo Zone debuted at number five on the US Billboard 200 with 87,000 album-equivalent units, including 83,000 pure album sales. It is the band's highest-charting album. The album debuted at number one on South Korea's Gaon Album Chart becoming the group's 5th launch to accomplish this feat. As of April 11, the album has sold 561,000 album-equivalent units worldwide.
